About Me

Product management, to me, is the discipline of making hard choices with incomplete information — and being accountable for them. I came to IPL's MBA programme with a burning curiosity about how India's best digital products are built, and I've spent every semester stress-testing frameworks against real companies.

I am drawn to the messy intersection of user psychology, business model design, and engineering constraints — the exact territory where great PMs live. My coursework spans innovation strategy, growth marketing, data analytics, and AI/ML foundations, each lens sharpening how I diagnose problems before jumping to solutions.

"The best PMs don't have the most ideas. They kill the most ideas — and ship the right ones."
